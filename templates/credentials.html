Copernicus DataSpace Ecosystem Credentials


For further analysis is necessary to set up the Copernicus DataSpace Ecosystem (CDSE) Credentials!


Copernicus DataSpace Ecosystem is a cloud-based platform that provides access to a wide range of Earth observation data and services. It enables users to discover, access, and analyze data from various sources, including satellite imagery, climate data, and more.

OpenEO Client Credentials provided by the Copernicus DataSpace Ecosystem enable access to data from the system and are essential for further analysis. Detailed information on Client Credentials and how to obtain them can be found here and here.
